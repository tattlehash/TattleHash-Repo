10. Gamified Fee Splitting

Gatekeeper and Enforced attestations may include optional gamified fee determination (Coin Toss).

  • Results are determined using provably fair blockchain randomness
  • Neither party can influence the outcome
  • The "sponsor" (party determined by the game) pays the full fee
  • The "sponsored" party pays nothing
  • Results are recorded in the attestation metadata and verifiable on-chain

There are no "winners" or "losers" - one party sponsors the attestation.

11. Downgrade Policy

If a Gatekeeper or Enforced attestation is initiated and the counterparty does not respond within 72 hours:

  • The attestation automatically downgrades to Fire
  • The initiating user receives a credit refund for the difference:
    • Gatekeeper to Fire: 3 credits refunded
    • Enforced to Fire: 9 credits refunded
  • The user retains Fire-level proof (notification sent, PDF generated)
