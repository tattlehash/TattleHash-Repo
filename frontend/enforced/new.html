Enforced Attestation

This is a closing room, not a negotiation table.

Enforced mode is designed for deals that have ALREADY been agreed in principle. Both parties arrive ready to formalize terms, review final documents, and commit.

If you're still negotiating price, scope, or key terms — do that first. Come back when you're ready to close.

Why attest a closed deal?
  • Creates permanent proof both parties agreed
  • Eliminates "that's not what we agreed to" disputes
  • Professional documentation of the transaction
  • Good business practice, not paranoia
